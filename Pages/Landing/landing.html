Open Source

Community-driven development. No pay-to-win, no ads, and a game that adapts and grows wth the community. Released under GPL-3.0 and CC-BY

Strategy Focused

While the game rules might be a little too much for the average gamer, we want a game that calls to the strategic and tactically mindful. And it's important that we allow room for growth through the development of alternative rulesets that can be generated in custom games.

Accessible

Clean and minimal, but endlessly customizable. We want to create an an easy to access web game that provides a quality experience for everyone.
