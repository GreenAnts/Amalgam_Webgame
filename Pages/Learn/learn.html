How to Play

The Objective

Amalgam is an abstract strategy game played on a unique board with golden lines connecting key intersections. Your goal is to either reach your opponent's goal line or eliminate their Void piece.

Core Pieces

The Amalgam

Your primary attacking piece. Moves along golden lines and eliminates adjacent enemy pieces.

The Void

Your defensive core. Amplifies gem abilities when adjacent. Protect it at all costs!

Portals

Special pieces that can swap positions with other pieces on golden lines.

Gem Abilities

Ruby Fireball

When two rubies align, unleash a devastating fireball that eliminates pieces in its path.

Pearl Tidal Wave

Aligned pearls create a sweeping wave that can eliminate multiple pieces in a direction.

Amber Sap

Trap and eliminate enemy pieces caught between two amber gems.

Jade Launch

Launch pieces across the board to strategic positions when jades align.

Golden Lines

The board features special golden lines that connect key intersections. Many pieces can only move along these golden paths, adding strategic depth to positioning and movement.

Master the golden line network to control the board and execute powerful combinations!
