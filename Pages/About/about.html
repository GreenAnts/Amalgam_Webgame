About Project

Amalgam

An open-source abstract strategy board game featuring unique mechanics, deep tactical gameplay, and a clean, distraction-free interface.

Project Philosophy

Amalgam was created with a simple goal: to provide a pure strategy gaming experience without ads, microtransactions, or artificial complexity. Every design decision prioritizes gameplay depth and player experience.

The game is released under open-source licenses (GPL-3.0 for code, CC-BY 4.0 for content) to ensure it remains free and community-driven forever.
